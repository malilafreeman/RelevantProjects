Let's learn how to take a good hike!

Step 1: bring the essentials.

  • Large water bottle
  • Pair of comfortable shoes
  • Snacks
  • Sunglasses
  • Rain protection

These items will keep you safe and comfortable on the trail.

Click here for more information on trail supplies.

Step 2: plan your route.

  1. Find your current location. Click here to find your location.
  2. Make sure the weather is good! Click here to check your local weather.
  3. Find a nearby hike. Make sure the mileage and difficuly level are good for you! AllTrails can help.

Step 3: Go enjoy your hike!

Here are some tips to make your experience even better:

  • Find a friend! Hiking is better with pals.
  • Explore new areas! You might find beautiful hikes in unexpected areas.
  • Always tell someone where you're headed! We hope you don't get lost, but it's always a possibility.
  • Get to the trailhead early! They fill up fast!
  • Bring a camera to snap photos of wildlife!
